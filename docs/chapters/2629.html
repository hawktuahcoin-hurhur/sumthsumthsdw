As the fiend fell to the ground, dozens of black chains burst from it and wrapped themselves around him. Individually, none of them were strong enough to bind him, but all together, they held long enough for more chains to descend on the struggling form and bury it under the mass of inky-black metal.

Soon, there was nothing but a small hill of black links towering where the horrid youth once stood, trembling slightly like a macabre cocoon.

A large swath of the Eternal City around them was entirely decimated...

But the ornate buildings were already restoring themselves, too.

Shattered statues coalesced into their original, elegant forms. Toppled pillars rose from the rubble and assembled themselves. The rubble floated into the air and returned to its previous shape.

The buildings grew from the devastated ruin like flowers, and the paving of the street smoothed itself out.

Before too long, Sunny and Jet found themselves standing on a beautiful plaza. Their surroundings were spotless and undamaged, with only the cocoon of black chains and their heavy breathing proving that the battle had happened.

Jet stared at the small hill of chains and then looked at Sunny with a somber expression.

"Hey."

He raised an eyebrow.

"What?"

She pointed to the black cocoon with the blade of her war scythe.

"That was just one of them, right?"

Sunny nodded slowly, prompting her to give him a dark smile.

"...And you said that there were thousands, or maybe even millions of these things within the city?"

He hesitated for a few moments, suppressing a shiver.

What could he say?

Sunny sighed.

"Sounds about right..."

As the person who had invited Jet to this nightmarish place, he was starting to feel a tiny bit guilty.
